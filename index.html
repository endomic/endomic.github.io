ENDOMIC Research Team

ENDOMIC (ENDOtyper les Maladies Inflammatoires Chroniques) specializes in precision medicine approaches using health data and artificial intelligence, contributing to the dynamics of our academic site within the France 2030 Excellence Initiative, which includes the University of Lille, Lille University Hospital, INSERM and INRIA. ENDOMIC is hosted by INFINITE (Institute for Translational Research in Inflammation) (Inserm U1286, University of Lille, CHU Lille).

Our team is also involved in an AI and Health Chair (ARCHIE) in partnership with KU Leuven and leads the HORIZON Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Thunder project.
