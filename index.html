ENDOMIC Research Team

ENDOMIC (ENDOtyper les Maladies Inflammatoires Chroniques) specializes in precision medicine approaches using health data and artificial intelligence, which is part of the dynamics of our academic site (France 2030 Excellence Initiative including the University of Lille, the Lille University Hospital, INSERM and INRIA).
